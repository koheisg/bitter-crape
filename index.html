Bitter Crape

A delightful crepe with a subtle bitterness that pairs perfectly with sweet toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, melted
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., chocolate, fruit, whipped cream)

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, milk, eggs, sugar, and salt until smooth.

  2. Step 2

    Stir in the melted butter.

  3. Step 3

    Heat a non-stick skillet or crepe pan over medium heat. Lightly grease the pan with butter or non-stick cooking spray.

  4. Step 4

    Pour about 1/4 cup of the batter into the pan, tilting the pan to evenly coat the bottom.

  5. Step 5

    Cook the crepe for 1-2 minutes, or until the edges start to curl and the center is set. Flip and cook for anoth

  6. Step 6

    Remove the crepe from the pan and repeat with the remaining batter, greasing the pan as needed.

  7. Step 7

    Serve the crepes warm, with your desired toppings.
